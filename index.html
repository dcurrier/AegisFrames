What you're testing

A serious catalog that respects your files, your hardware, and your time.

The beta focuses on the core workflow: bring your RAW files in, organize them fast, and know they're safe. Here's where your feedback matters most.

01 — SPEED

Fast where it counts

Import, thumbnail, and browse large RAW catalogs without the spinning-beachball tax. A native Mac app tuned for how photographers actually move through a shoot.

02 — STORAGE

Your library lives where you want

Store the catalog on an external or network drive and pick up on another Mac. Designed for photographers who don't keep everything on the boot volume.

03 — RESILIENCE

Disaster recovery, built in

RAW files are treated as immutable originals and never edited in place. Backups run to the destinations you choose, so a lost drive doesn't mean a lost archive — and a catalog can be rebuilt from the files themselves.

04 — OWNERSHIP

No subscription bloat

An affordable Mac-native alternative to the rent-forever model. You'll help shape a tool built to be owned, not endlessly leased.

The beta program
